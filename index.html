Recipe

Chocolate Cake

"This chocolate cake recipe is a classic. This version of the recipe is fast, quick, and your kids will love it!"

Difficulty Preparation Cooking Total Time
Easy 10 min 25 min 35 min

Ingredients

Black Chocolate: 200g

Sugar: 150g

Butter: 200g

Eggs: 4

Flour: 80g

Instructions

1

 Melt the chocolate and the butter together

2

 Mix the sugar, eggs and the flour

3

 Mix everything to get a smooth dough

4

 Bake in the oven
